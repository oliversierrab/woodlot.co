# Woodlot Service Documentation

Woodlot is a family-owned Miami carpentry company providing custom cabinetry
manufacturing and millwork solutions for South Florida homes and businesses.

Canonical website: https://www.woodlot.co/

## Services

- Custom kitchen cabinets
- Custom closets
- Custom vanities
- Custom wall units
- Custom furniture and millwork consultations

## Service Area

Woodlot serves South Florida, including Miami-Dade County, Miramar, Miami,
Kendall, Hollywood, Hialeah, Fort Lauderdale, Davie, Coral Springs, and Cooper
City.
