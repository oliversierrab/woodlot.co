Welcome to Woodlot

Custom millwork that starts with how you live.

Woodlot is a Miami-based team of skilled artisans serving homes and businesses across South Florida. Every project balances functional storage, refined proportions, and the finish choices that make a room feel personal.

From compact kitchens to one-of-a-kind furniture pieces, the work is shaped around your vision, your dimensions, and the materials that will hold up beautifully over time.
